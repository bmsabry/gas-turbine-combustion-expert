Conclusions
z Driver for gasification and IGCC in Western Canada is 
conversion of low coal and oil sands residues into clean power, 
hydrogen and high value products while capturing CO2
z RD&D to improve overall performance and cost 
competitiveness include
– Advanced gasifiers and gas turbines 
– Advanced gas cleanup technologies
– Membranes for producing oxygen and separating hydrogen & CO2
– Advanced CO2 capture technologies
– Integration and optimization of all system components. 
z First of a kind demonstration plants are needed to accelerate 
commercial acceptance and deployment.
