Combustion Dynamics Nomenclature
Combustion Dynamics Nomenclature
• Many common terms for combustion dynamics
• Dynamics, Humming, Rumble, Oscillations, Pulsations, Instability, 
Screech

• Rumble, Cold Tone, Helmholtz Mode
• Mid Frequency Dynamics (MFD or IFD)
• Hum, Hot Tone, Longitudinal Mode
• High Frequency Dynamics (HFD)
• Screech, Transverse instability
• Very destructive
